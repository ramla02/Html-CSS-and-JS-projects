Coconut Curry Chicken

oconut Curry Chicken recipe is a delicious and easy way to add spice to your weeknight dinner routine. Made with tender chicken, coconut cream, and flavorful spices, this dish is one of my favourite.

Ingredients

  • coconut oil
  • boneless chicken
  • yellow onion
  • tomato paste
  • garlic and ginger
  • curry powder
  • paprika
  • black pepper
  • full-fat coconut cream
  • broth (optional) of your choice
  • a mixed spices of your choice

Instructions

  1. In a large souce pan brown the chicken pieces in coconut oil for about 5 to 8 minutes. Then add the onions and saute together.
  2. Next, add the ginger and garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. then Add the spices of your choice and stir to blend everything together.
  4. Finally, add the broth, tomato paste, and coconut cream. Simmer over low heat for 20 minutes. Serve over rice (optional).
